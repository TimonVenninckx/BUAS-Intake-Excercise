
Angry Faces.

The game can be played using the mouse/touchpad only.

You can launch the player character form the catapult by holding mouse button on it,
and dragging it away from the catapult.

You can choose a level by clicking on the level icons(1,2,3,...)
Quitting the level can be done by clicking on the main menu button
or pressing escape on your keyboard.

You gain score by destroying objects enemy characters give high score.

The goal is to kill all the enemy sad faces in the game.
In the top left you can see how many lives you have left for the current level.
